ABOUT US

Welcome to Susee Beauty Saloon and Spa, your ultimate destination for all your beauty needs. We are a full-service beauty salon and spa, providing a range of skincare services, including acne scar removal, age marks treatment, and facials. At Susee Beauty Saloon and Spa, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional beauty services to our clients. Our team of experienced beauty professionals is dedicated to helping you achieve your desired look, whether it's for a special occasion or just for your everyday life

In addition to our beauty services, we also offer training for students who want to learn the latest beauty techniques. Our courses cover a wide range of topics, including cosmetology, trichology, aesthetics, and laser studio training. Our goal is to equip our students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the beauty industry. At Susee Beauty Saloon and Spa, we use only the highest-quality products and equipment to ensure that our clients receive the best possible results. Our team is constantly updating our techniques and staying up-to-date with the latest trends and developments in the beauty industry.
